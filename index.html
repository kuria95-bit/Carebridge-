Discover CareBridge

We partner with families and expert professionals to deliver personalized, evidence-based support for children with autism and developmental needs, helping every child reach their full potential.

About CareBridge

CareBridge is committed to empowering families by linking them with highly qualified expert professionals in autism spectrum disorders and developmental therapies. Headquartered in Nairobi, Kenya, we offer comprehensive, evidence-based services designed to enhance child development, promote independence, and support family well-being through a network of vetted specialists.

Our Services

Our expert-led, evidence-based therapies are tailored to nurture growth, independence, and family harmony.

Speech Therapy

Personalized sessions to enhance communication skills and expression.

Applied Behavior Analysis (ABA)

Structured approaches to foster positive behaviors and reduce challenges.

Occupational Therapy

Building daily living skills for greater autonomy and confidence.

Physical Therapy

Improving motor skills and physical coordination through targeted exercises.

Sensory Integration Therapy

Helping children process sensory information for better focus and calm.

Social Skills Training

Developing interpersonal skills for meaningful connections and interactions.

Music Therapy

Using music to support emotional expression and cognitive development.
