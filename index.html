About Us

Unlimited Possibilities Africa is a community-rooted organization helping children with autism access quality, compassionate care — regardless of family income.

Our Mission

To connect families with vetted therapists and create inclusive communities where every child can thrive.

Our Approach

Family-first, evidence-informed, culturally sensitive — we work with caregivers and local partners to deliver sustainable impact.

Our Values

Trust, compassion, transparency, inclusion and measurable outcomes.

Services

Evidence-based supports for speech, behavior, daily living, social skills and community integration.

Speech & Language Therapy

Playful, effective sessions to support communication development.

Behavioral Support (ABA)

Compassionate, structured approaches to learning and behaviour change.

Daily & Occupational Skills

Practical therapy to build independence in daily life.

How it works — for parents

A simple, three-step process so you get the right support quickly.

1. Apply

Complete a short form about your child — concerns, goals and availability.

2. Match

Our team matches you with vetted professionals who fit your needs and schedule.

3. Start & Track

Begin therapy, attend workshops, and track progress with our support team.
