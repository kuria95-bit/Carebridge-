How it works

1. Quick sign-up

Parents create a short profile describing needs. Experts submit credentials and availability.

2. Smart matching

Match by expertise, location, language, availability and family preferences. Option to request subsidised care.

3. Connect & get help

Secure messaging, video sessions, session notes & follow-ups. Donors may sponsor families directly through the platform.
