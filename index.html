About Our Platform

We are a technology-driven, community-rooted platform that connects parents to vetted autism specialists. Our mission is to make quality, evidence-informed care accessible — regardless of income or location.

We are matchmakers

We don't provide therapy directly. Our strength is matching families to the best local or remote clinicians based on needs, age, language and availability.

Quality & safety

Clinicians undergo background checks, credential verification and reference checks before joining our network. Ongoing reviews keep quality high.

Parent-centered approach

We support caregivers with clear guidance, progress tracking and workshops so gains are sustainable at home and school.

Equity & community

Through donor partnerships we subsidize therapy places for families in low-income communities and run community events and training.
