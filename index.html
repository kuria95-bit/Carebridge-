Unlimited Possibilities for Children with Autism

A trusted platform connecting parents to vetted therapists, special educators, behaviour analysts and support services across Africa.

  • • Vetted professionals with verified credentials
  • • Accessible telehealth & in-person options
  • • Sliding-scale & donor-funded sessions

How it works

1. Create a profile

Parents and experts create simple profiles. Experts upload qualifications for verification.

2. Match & book

Smart filters match your needs and budget. Book telehealth or in-person sessions.

3. Support & measure progress

Track sessions, outcome measures, and access donor-funded slots for low-income families.
