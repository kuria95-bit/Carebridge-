About Our Platform

We assist parents to find and connect with carefully vetted professionals — therapists and clinicians who provide speech, behavioral, occupational and psychosocial supports for children with autism.

Professional Matching

Algorithm + clinician review ensures you get matches who fit your child's goals, culture and schedule.

Community & Donor Support

We run workshops and partner with donors to subsidize care for low-income families.

Services Provided

Clinicians on our platform offer a range of evidence-informed supports. Pick a service to learn more or request a match.

Speech & Language Therapy

Engaging, play-based sessions to improve communication and social interaction.

Group Social Skills

Small-group practice for peers to build interaction and play skills.

Occupational & Daily Skills

Practical therapy for independence in dressing, eating and classroom routines.

Parent Coaching

Short, practical coaching sessions for caregivers with home-based strategies.

Teletherapy

Remote sessions for families in remote or underserved areas.

Donor-Supported Aid

Donors sponsor materials, sessions and events for families who cannot otherwise afford therapy.
