About CareBridge

CareBridge is a product of Unlimited Possibilities Africa (UPA), built to make it easier for parents of children with autism to access trusted experts online — without the hustle. Starting in Kenya, we are building the bridge between families and professionals, ensuring every child gets the right care at the right time.
