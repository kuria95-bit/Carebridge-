About Unlimited Possibilities Africa

Unlimited Possibilities Africa (UPA) is a Kenya-based platform bridging the gap between families of children with autism and qualified professionals. We connect parents to trusted experts, empower specialists with visibility, and ensure no family is left behind — starting in Kenya, with a vision to expand across Africa.
